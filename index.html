Questions you might be asked.

Do I have to log every trip?

No. Trip logging is optional. You can still track a full shift without it.

What platforms does Safi support?

Any platform where you use your car to earn, including Uber, Careem, and Bolt.

How are fuel and oil costs calculated?

Safi estimates costs using your vehicle details and tracked distance/time.
